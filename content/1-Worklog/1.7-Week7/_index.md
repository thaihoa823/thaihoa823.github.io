### Week 7 Objectives:

* Learn important AWS security principles, including defense in depth and least privilege.

* Become familiar with identity management, threat detection, and data encryption services.

* Understand how to protect credentials, passwords, and sensitive configuration information.

### Week 7 Achievements:

* Gained a clear understanding of defense in depth and the principle of least privilege on AWS.

* Became familiar with GuardDuty and Security Hub for detecting and managing security findings.

* Distinguished between Cognito User Pools, Identity Pools, and IAM Identity Center.

* Applied AWS KMS and ACM concepts to protect stored data and network communications.

* Used Parameter Store and Secrets Manager concepts to avoid storing sensitive information directly in source code.

* Completed the security configuration review and removed unused practice resources.

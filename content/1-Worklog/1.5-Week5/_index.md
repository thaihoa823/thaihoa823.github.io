### Week 5 Objectives:

* Learn about relational and NoSQL database services on AWS.

* Distinguish between the roles of Amazon RDS, Amazon Aurora, and Amazon DynamoDB.

* Become familiar with Amazon ElastiCache and Amazon Redshift for performance improvement and data analytics.

### Week 5 Achievements:

* Distinguished between relational and NoSQL databases on AWS.

* Gained a clear understanding of the roles of Amazon RDS, Aurora, DynamoDB, ElastiCache, and Redshift.

* Successfully created an Amazon RDS MySQL database and configured basic access permissions.

* Applied DB Snapshots for backup and reviewed the database recovery process.

* Completed the creation of a DynamoDB table with a Partition Key, Sort Key, and sample data.

* Became familiar with the On-demand and Provisioned Capacity modes of DynamoDB.

* Applied the acquired knowledge to select suitable database services for different requirements.

* Checked and removed practice resources after completing the tasks.
